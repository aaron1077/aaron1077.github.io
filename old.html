Vaporizers

Box Mods

Box mods are great because they generate a lot of vape and a strong hit.
Most box mods also come with a screen to adjust the wattage.
Although, some box mods are big and uncomfortable to carry.


Cylinder Mods

Cylinder mods are my favorite because they can give just as much wattage and hit power that
a box mod does, but they fit easier in your hand are easier to carry because of their shape.
They also have a display screen to adjust wattage so they are easily my favorite in my opinion.


Mechanical Mods

Mechanical mods are mods that you build yourself. Your able to build the mod to your perfect liking.
The problem with mechanical mods is if you don't know what you are doing
you can cause a lot of issues to your device. Some peoples mechanical mods explode because they built it wrong.
I advice you not to build one unless you have a good understanding of how to do it correctly.
